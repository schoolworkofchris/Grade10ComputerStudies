High-End PC

If you want a powerful PC for gaming but don’t need the best parts and don’t want to spend thousands of dollars then this PC is for you. Even though this PC doesn’t use the ultimate parts, this PC is still going to be very capable and powerful and will be able to handle 4K and possibly even some 8K video editing, this PC will also be able to handle most games at well over 100 FPS. just be warned that while it will cost significantly less than the ultimate, this PC will still cost you well over $1000.00


Parts in This Build

  • Ryzen 7 3700X
  • RTX 3070
  • GIGABYTE B365M DS3H
  • Corsair Vengeance LPX 16GB
  • WD_Black SN750 1TB NVMe Internal Gaming SSD
  • Corsair CX Series 750 Watt ATX/EPS Modular 80 Plus Bronze
  • NZXT H510i - Compact ATX Mid -Tower PC Gaming Case

Also, feel free to change some of the parts in this build to your liking if you want, you could even throw in a graphics card to get more frames in gaming.
