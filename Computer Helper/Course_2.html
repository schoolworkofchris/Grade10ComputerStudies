Form Factor

Ok, so now its time to choose your form factor, there are mainly two main form factors which this tutorial is going to go into, there are desktop computers, and then there are the laptops which are great for their portability.


This is the point where you have to decide which form factor is best for you or you won’t be able to carry in with this tutorial, below is a list of pros and cons of each.


Desktop PC:

Pro Con
Can be upgraded way more than a laptop Not portable
The larger size means more power Must be plugged in (no battery), this is an issue when you have a power outage
Can be built yourself Needs other peripherals which aren’t included (screen, mouse, keyboard)

Laptop:

Pro Con
Very portable Less upgradability (very limited)
Has a battery Less powerful than a PC in its price range
Has wifi card (many PC’s require ethernet unless you install a wifi card) Ethernet is faster than wifi
