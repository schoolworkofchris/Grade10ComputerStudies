Processor / CPU (The Brainz)

To start the journey through your PC build let’s start by picking one of the most important parts of your PC and that would be the processor, what is a processor? well you may know it better by the name “CPU”, and the CPU is the brains of the computer, it is what gives the computer functionality, it calculates what needs to be calculated and displays the text as the text we all know and love rather than the 1’s and 0’s that the processor knows.


There are two main processor manufacturers that all people use in their computers and you may even have an older processor from one of the manufacturers, these manufactures are AMD and Intel, at the moment AMD is better but these stats always change so I recommend finding an up to date article on which company is better first, but since AMD is better at the time of me making this course, I will be referring to them more.


But now that we know what two companies you should be buying from, let’s take a look at the processor lineups from both of these companies.
