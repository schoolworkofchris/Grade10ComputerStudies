AMD

Lets first talk AMD, AMD calls their lineups of CPU’s Ryzen, AMD has CPU’s for multiple markets, there is entry-level (budget and least powerful), main-stream (mid-range, most popular), performance (powerful CPU’s), and last would be High-end (creme de la creme from AMD).


Entry Level:

CPU Name Cores (threads)
Ryzen 3 3100 4 (8)
Ryzen 3 3100x 4 (8)

Main-Stream

CPU Name Cores (threads)
Ryzen 5 5600x 6 (12)

Performance

CPU Name Cores (threads)
Ryzen 5 5800x 8 (16)

High-End

CPU Name Cores (threads)
Ryzen 9 5900x 12 (24)
Ryzen 9 5950x 16 (32)

Note: these CPU’s are the newest available on the market from AMD, but you may not need the newest depending on how you are planning on using your new computer, getting an older generation of these CPU’s can save you quite a bit.
